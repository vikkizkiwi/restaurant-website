About Us

Founded in 1999, Feane Restaurant started as a small family diner with a big dream: to serve the best burgers and fries in town. Over the years, we’ve expanded our menu to include pizzas, wraps, and wings, but our mission has stayed the same: fresh ingredients, delicious food, and happy customers. We believe in good food made with love and enjoyed with friends and family.
